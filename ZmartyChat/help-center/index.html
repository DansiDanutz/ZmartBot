Getting Started with ZmartyChat

Last updated: 2 days ago â€¢ 5 min read

Welcome to ZmartyChat! This guide will help you get started with our AI-powered cryptocurrency trading platform.

1. Create Your Account

Sign up for a ZmartyChat account in just a few steps:

  1. Visit zmartychat.com/signup
  2. Enter your email and create a strong password
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile information
Pro Tip: Use a unique password and enable two-factor authentication for maximum security.

2. Complete KYC Verification

To comply with regulations and ensure platform security, we require identity verification:

  • Upload a government-issued ID
  • Provide proof of address
  • Take a selfie for identity confirmation

3. Fund Your Account

Add funds to start trading:

  • Bank Transfer: 1-3 business days
  • Credit/Debit Card: Instant
  • Cryptocurrency: 10-60 minutes

4. Configure AI Providers

Choose which AI providers you want to use for market analysis:
